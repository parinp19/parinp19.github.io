Green Salsa

condiments

  • 2 ​Tomatillos
  • 1 ​Jalapeño
  • 1/2 a Red Onion
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 cup of cilatro
  • A pinch of salt
  1. Cut the tomatillos and jalapeno in half, and cut the onion into 1/4 inch rounds
  2. Lay onto a baking sheet with the garlic cloves and Broil at 450 degrees for 5-10 minutes until a light char
  3. Add to a blender along with the cilatro and lemon, blend until desired consistency
